Data Control

Multiple data files from the Top 200 weekly are compiled into a playlist Playlist is in ascending order which is ordered using a recursive merge sort. Press "H" to see the history of all songs they have listened to starting from the the most recent using a single linked list. Press "E" to exit the playlist and end the program.
